non-academic background

I started my musical journey back in high school when I picked up the flute. It quickly became a passion, and I joined the COAR Lima band, where I had the opportunity to perform and learn as part of a talented group. While the flute remains my favorite instrument, I also have explored others, including the acoustic guitar, piano, violin, and recorder (just as a hobby). Now, back at Dartmouth, I'm part of the Dartmouth Chamber Orchestra (DCO), continuing to have fun and grow musically!

Besides that, I enjoy journaling and capturing my day-to-day life through doodles. It's a creative way for me to reflect and express my thoughts and experiences. Also, I enjoy doing video and image editing (and other random stuff with friends). I have edited/made all the videos below! You can and take a look at my highlights to know more about my daily life.
